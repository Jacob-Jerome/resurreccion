Hello, my name is Jacob Jerome V. Resurreccion, and I am now a first-year student at Far Eastern University. I am pursuing a degree in Information Technology with a specialty in Cybersecurity (BSITCST), which has always been a fascinating area to me. My interest for technology and cybersecurity leads me to always seek education and keep up with the newest breakthroughs in the field. I currently live in Marilao, Bulacan, although my education began in Valenzuela City. I graduated from senior high school at Our Lady of Fatima University, with honors for my academic achievements. Although I don't consider myself a particularly social person, I have gained particular intrapersonal skills. This quality has been extremely useful in both my academic and personal life, helping me to form strong, supportive relationships. In addition to my interpersonal skills, I have a strong technical ability. I've always been captivated by the complexity of phones and computers, and I understand their components and functions. This skill set not only helps me with my education, but it also allows me to assist others in resolving technology issues. Furthermore, I take interest in my capacity to adapt. I have a tendency for turning difficult situations into opportunities to develop successful solutions. This resilience has helped me overcome a variety of challenges, both intellectual and personal. Overall, my path has been defined by a combination of academic excellence, technological mastery, and excellent interpersonal abilities. As I continue my studies at FEU, I am excited to further improve these skills and make a significant contribution to the subject of cybersecurity.
